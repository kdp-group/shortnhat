Who We Are

Shortnhat reimagines the centuries-old craft of Aṣọ Òkè weaving, blending time-honoured technique with contemporary silhouettes. Every pair of trousers is a conversation between past and present, hand-woven by Yoruba artisans and tailored for global streets.

Our Philosophy

Our Vision

To lead a new wave of Afrocentric fashion by making Aṣọ Òkè a staple in everyday wear — from street style to luxury fashion.

Our Mission
